## Domain Details

### Phase 27: Aesthetics & Value
- **Core**: Beauty, art, taste
- **Key Questions**: What is beauty? What is art? Is taste objective?
- **Traditions**: Kant, Hume, Danto, Goodman
- **Q-Score Mapping**: PHI (HARMONY, ELEGANCE), BURN (VALUE_CREATION)

### Phase 28: Philosophy of Mind
- **Core**: Consciousness, intentionality, mental states
- **Key Questions**: What is consciousness? How do mind and body relate?
- **Traditions**: Descartes, Dennett, Chalmers, Nagel
- **Q-Score Mapping**: PHI (COHERENCE), CULTURE (AUTHENTICITY, RESONANCE)

### Phase 29: Philosophy of Language
- **Core**: Meaning, reference, speech acts
- **Key Questions**: What is meaning? How do words refer?
- **Traditions**: Frege, Wittgenstein, Austin, Kripke
- **Q-Score Mapping**: VERIFY (ACCURACY), PHI (PRECISION)

### Phase 30: Philosophy of Action
- **Core**: Agency, intention, free will
- **Key Questions**: What is an action? Are we free?
- **Traditions**: Davidson, Frankfurt, Bratman
- **Q-Score Mapping**: BURN (UTILITY, EFFICIENCY), VERIFY (INTEGRITY)

### Phase 31: Social & Political Philosophy
- **Core**: Justice, rights, legitimacy
- **Key Questions**: What is justice? What legitimizes authority?
- **Traditions**: Rawls, Nozick, Habermas, Sen
- **Q-Score Mapping**: CULTURE (IMPACT, ALIGNMENT), VERIFY (TRANSPARENCY)

### Phase 32: Philosophy of Science
- **Core**: Scientific method, explanation, theory change
- **Key Questions**: What is science? How do theories change?
- **Traditions**: Popper, Kuhn, Lakatos, van Fraassen
- **Q-Score Mapping**: VERIFY (VERIFIABILITY, REPRODUCIBILITY, ACCURACY)

### Phase 33: Metaphysics II
- **Core**: Identity, causation, time
- **Key Questions**: What makes things identical? What is causation?
- **Traditions**: Lewis, Kripke, Parfit, McTaggart
- **Q-Score Mapping**: PHI (COHERENCE, STRUCTURE, COMPLETENESS)

### Phase 34: Philosophy of Religion
- **Core**: Theism, evil, faith and reason
- **Key Questions**: Does God exist? Why is there evil?
- **Traditions**: Aquinas, Plantinga, Mackie, Hick
- **Q-Score Mapping**: CULTURE (AUTHENTICITY, RESONANCE)

### Phase 35: Meta-Philosophy
- **Core**: Philosophical method, progress, integration
- **Key Questions**: What is philosophy? Does it make progress?
- **Traditions**: Williamson, Cappelen, Chalmers
- **Q-Score Mapping**: PHI (COHERENCE, COMPLETENESS)

### Phase 36: Applied Ethics
- **Core**: Bioethics, environmental ethics, tech ethics
- **Key Questions**: What is ethical in medicine, technology, environment?
- **Traditions**: Singer, Jonas, Floridi
- **Q-Score Mapping**: BURN (SUSTAINABILITY, NON_EXTRACTIVE), CULTURE (IMPACT)

### Phase 37: Eastern Philosophy
- **Core**: Buddhist, Daoist, Vedanta traditions
- **Key Questions**: What is self? What is enlightenment?
- **Traditions**: Nagarjuna, Laozi, Shankara
- **Q-Score Mapping**: CULTURE (AUTHENTICITY, ALIGNMENT, RESONANCE), PHI (HARMONY)

### Phase 38: Continental Philosophy
- **Core**: Phenomenology, existentialism, critical theory
- **Key Questions**: What is experience? What is authentic existence?
- **Traditions**: Husserl, Heidegger, Sartre, Habermas
- **Q-Score Mapping**: CULTURE (AUTHENTICITY, RESONANCE), VERIFY (PROVENANCE)

### Phase 39: Formal Philosophy
- **Core**: Modal logic, decision theory, game theory
- **Key Questions**: What is possible? How should we decide?
- **Traditions**: Kripke, Savage, von Neumann
- **Q-Score Mapping**: PHI (STRUCTURE, PRECISION, COHERENCE)

### Phase 40: CYNIC Synthesis
- **Core**: Integration, synthesis, φ-completeness
- **Key Questions**: How do all domains connect?
- **Traditions**: CYNIC's own
- **Q-Score Mapping**: All axioms unified

### Phase 41: Philosophy of Mathematics
- **Core**: Foundations, ontology, practice
- **Key Questions**: What are numbers? Is math discovered or invented?
- **Traditions**: Frege, Gödel, Lakatos
- **Q-Score Mapping**: PHI (STRUCTURE, PRECISION, COMPLETENESS)

### Phase 42: Pragmatism & Process
- **Core**: Peirce, James, Dewey, Whitehead
- **Key Questions**: What works? What is inquiry?
- **Traditions**: Classical American pragmatism
- **Q-Score Mapping**: BURN (UTILITY, EFFICIENCY), CULTURE (RELEVANCE)

### Phase 43: Global Philosophy

### Phase 44: Philosophy of Law & Economics

- **Traditions**: Hart, Dworkin, Posner, Sen
- **Q-Score Mapping**: BURN (UTILITY, VALUE_CREATION, EFFICIENCY)
